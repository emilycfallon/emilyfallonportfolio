About Me

A little bit about myself...

Welcome! I am a twenty-one year old, self taught, frontend web developer based in Nashua, NH. When I am not behind a computer screen, you can find me obsessing over my golden retriever, cooking, dancing (sometimes seperately, sometimes simultaneously) and as of recently, choreographing musical theatre shows. My dream is to see every corner of the world.

My coding journey has been short yet impactful. In only a few months, I have developed a knack for responsive design. With my proficient skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript, I have been able to create webpages that are visually appealing and receptive.

I take pride in being a self-directed learner. My instinctual curiousity mixed with an addiction to problem-solving, I have become obsessed with teaching myself new things. I learn something new everyday, and cannot wait to share that knowledge with you.
